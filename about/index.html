About me

TL;DR - Because why should I make you scroll down more than you need to?

I'm a student at MakerSquare LA, part of Hack Reactor Core. And these posts are a collection of my thoughts.

No need to Google me, here's everything you need to know about me

I was born and raised just outside the Chicago, and paid a lot of out-of-state tuition at the University of Wisconsin-Madison to meet one priceless girl who I would eventually marry. I will travel anywhere to try good, local eats and the occasional kicker (Tacos de Lengua, anyone?). Film or digital, it doesn't matter; I love all types of photos. I Amazon Prime everything, thumbs down every Phoenix song on Pandora, and drink my coffee black. Not to mention, I spend way too much time and money in coffee shops.

From 2011-2012, I studied as a MEXT Scholar (Monbukagakusho) at Keio University. In 2013 I graduated and moved to Richmond, VA. There, I work worked as an Associate Consultant at The Frontier Project. After 2 years, I resigned from my position and moved out West with my wife to pursue a different career path.

So here I am in Los Angeles, CA, currently preparing to attend MakerSquare for the November 16th cohort.
